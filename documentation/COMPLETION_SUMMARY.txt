## 💰 Cost Structure

### API Pricing
- **Embeddings**: $0.0001 per 1K tokens
- **GPT-3.5**: ~$0.0015 per 1K tokens
- **Per query**: ~$0.003 average

### Monthly Estimates
| Usage | Cost |
|-------|------|
| Light (5 chats) | $0.08 |
| Medium (15 chats) | $0.46 |
| Heavy (50 chats) | $2.30 |

### Cost Optimization
- Reduce chunk retrieval (3 → 2)
- Shorter documents
- Cache embeddings
- Batch processing
